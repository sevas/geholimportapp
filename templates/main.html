About

GeHOL is the scheduling system used at Brussels University.
Gehol Importer is a tool to convert GeHOL queries into something usable by calendar softwares such as Google Calendar, Apple iCal, Mozilla Sunbird, Microsoft Outlook.

This service is still under development. We will try to keep the core functionality running, but you are likely to experience changes as we add features and improve usability.
